Arakkonam

Arakkonam - My Home Town

Location: Arakkonam is in Ranipet district, Tamil Nadu. Distance from major places: About 69 km from Chennai by rail. Status: It is a municipality, and since 2022 it is included within the Chennai Metropolitan Area. Railway: Arakkonam Junction (AJJ) is a major rail junction in Southern Railway. It is strategically located at the intersection of the Chennai–Bangalore line and Guntakal–Chennai line (part of the Mumbai–Chennai route). There is an Engineering Workshop (EWS) and Electric Loco Shed (ELS) for the railways there. INS Rajali, a naval air base at Arakkonam, has a runway of 4,500 meters, one of the longest in South Asia. Industry: Some industrial units are present—MRF Tyres, Ramco Industries, UltraTech Cement. Market & Commerce: The municipal market (near the railway station) is a central commercial hub with many shops; commercial activities are also along Gandhi Road near the old bus stand.
